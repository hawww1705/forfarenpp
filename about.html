Our History

Milestones & Timeline

2011 • Foundation

Mada Global Established

Mada Global began in Semarang, Central Java as a civil consultancy team, specializing in structural loading calculations, quantity surveying, and small-scale residential renovations.

2015 • Expansion

General Contracting Licence

Acquired formal general contracting permits, expanding operations into building medium-sized commercial shops, cafes, and industrial warehousing projects.

2019 • Synergy

IF Interior Project Launch

Recognizing the client's desire for seamless transitions, IF Interior Project was launched under the same owner. We established a dedicated 800 sqm furniture fabrication workshop in Semarang.

2023 • Maturity

Luxury Portfolios

Completed our 100th project, serving premium commercial office spaces in Semarang, luxury beachfront villa structures, and bespoke residential cabinetry contracts across Central Java and Jakarta.
